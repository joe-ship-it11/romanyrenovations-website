The Founder

A Career Built
One Joint at a Time.

"I'd rather build one kitchen properly than three kitchens that fall apart in five years."

As the founder of Romany Renovations, I bring a unique blend of craftsmanship and artistic vision. With a background in music composition and conducting, along with interior design expertise, I approach every renovation as an art form.

After 15 years in Canada, my work reflects both hands-on craftsmanship — having personally completed homes, villas, and commercial spaces — and a deep appreciation for aesthetics. Romany Renovations is where practical precision meets artistic expression.
